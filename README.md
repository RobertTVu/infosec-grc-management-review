# InfoSec GRC — Management Review & ISMS Maturity Assessment
 
A group case study for a fictional medtech company, carried out as a **management review** (ISO/IEC 27001, clause 9.3) using the **MSB Metodstöd** and the **Mognadsdialogen** maturity model. I led the group work.

---

## Overview
 
The task: assess the company's current information security maturity, identify gaps, and present prioritised improvement proposals to a simulated management team - the way a real *ledningens genomgång* (management review) is conducted under ISO 27001.
 
Context that shaped the analysis: the company operates toward a public authority, which brings it into scope for **NIS2**, and it handles personal data under **GDPR**.
 
---
 
## What we did
 
- **Maturity assessment** of the ISMS using the Mognadsdialogen (four levels across six perspectives)
- **Gap analysis** - current state vs. desired state
- **Risk assessment** and outline of a risk treatment plan (required by both the Metodstöd and ISO 27001)
- **Regulatory mapping** - GDPR and NIS2 applicability
- **Improvement proposals** - what to do, how, and who owns it
- **Example governance document** (*styrdokument*) showing structure and intent
- **Presentation** to a simulated management team

## The PDCA / ISMS cycle
 
The work follows the Plan–Do–Check–Act cycle that the ISMS is built on. The management review sits in the *Act* phase.
 
<img width="300" height="190" alt="PDCA" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10ab43a6-f7a6-4f53-98f4-cc8b13583639" />


- **Plan** — risk, context, gap analysis, business analysis
- **Do** — objectives, governance documents, information classification, training
- **Check** — monitor, audit, evaluate
- **Act** — management review (ISO 27001, clause 9.3)

 
---
 
## Maturity model (Mognadsdialogen)
 
Four maturity levels, assessed across six perspectives:
 
| Level | Name | Meaning |
|-------|------|---------|
| 1 | Reactive | Ad hoc, no systematic approach |
| 2 | Some structure | Partial routines, inconsistent |
| 3 | Improvement-driven | Systematic, measured, improving |
| 4 | Proactive | Embedded, continuously optimised |
 
**Perspectives assessed:** risk management · information classification · incident handling · procurement · competence · follow-up.
